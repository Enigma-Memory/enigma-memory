Enigma creates and verifies Enigma-controlled local receipts. It does not claim that AI providers delete their own logs, backups, or model memories.

What Enigma does

One local vault

Enigma keeps its canonical memory vault on your device. No cloud account or provider credential required for the default desktop path.

Connect your assistants

Detect installed AI clients, preview the setting Enigma will change, and approve the connection. Claude Desktop, Cursor, Kimi Code, and more.

Health and proof

Check vault and connection readiness from the health dashboard. Export public-safe proof artifacts without exposing raw memory text.
