Privacy

Your Memory Drive starts local.

Enigma keeps Memory Drive data on your device and only shares selected context with an AI client when you choose to connect and use it.

See the setup flow

What stays local

  • Your Memory Drive is stored in app-managed local storage on your device.
  • Receipts, roots, and checkpoints are computed from local Memory Drive events.
  • Health and diagnostic data are derived from local metadata only.

What connected AI clients may receive

  • When you use a connected AI client, Enigma may send selected context from your Memory Drive.
  • You approve which clients are connected and what kind of context they can request.
  • Connected clients may keep their own logs, caches, and retention policies.

What Enigma cannot promise about third-party providers

AI providers may have separate logs, retention, account settings, caches, or native memory outside Enigma control. Removing memory from Enigma-controlled context is not the same as proving anything about a provider's own stored copies or model state.

Public-safe proofs

Proof artifacts can safely show hashes, roots, opaque references, counts, timestamps, signatures, and validation results. They should not reveal private memory text, conversation content, completions, embeddings, provider output, or provider-specific identifiers.

Boundary note: Enigma proves Memory Drive and receipt events. It does not prove anything about provider-side copies, provider-native recall stores, model state, or compliance status.
